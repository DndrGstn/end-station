“På spåret” (which means “On the Track”) is a popular Swedish TV quiz show. Two teams compete against each other. They are “traveling” by train across the world (virtually). Each team is given clues about a city or country. They get points for guessing the correct place — the earlier they guess, the more points they get. The show combines geography, culture, and trivia with a fun travel theme. Think of it as a mix between a geography quiz and a travel game.

Game Rules

  • Press Start Game to begin.
  • One clue is shown at a time.
  • Press STOP to stop the train and enter your guess.
  • You get fewer points for each additional clue.
  • Have fun!
